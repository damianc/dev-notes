# Constructing a Box

$$
\begin{array}{ll}
V(x) & = (a-2x)^2x
\\
& = (a^2 + 4x^2 - 4ax)x
\\
& = a^2x + 4x^3 - 4ax^2
\\
& = 4x^3 - 4ax^2 + a^2x
\\\ \\
V'(x) & = 12x^2 - 8ax + a^2
\end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l}
A = 12
\ 
B = -8a
\ 
C = a^2
\\
\Delta = B^2 - 4AC & = 64a^2 - 4 \cdot 12 \cdot a^2
\\
& = 64a^2 - 48a^2
\\
& = 16a^2
\\
\sqrt{\Delta} = \sqrt{16a^2}
& =
\sqrt{16} \cdot \sqrt{a^2} = 4a
\\\ \\
x_{1,2} = \frac{-B \pm \sqrt{\Delta}}{2A}
\\
x_1 = \frac{8a+4a}{24}
& = \frac{12a}{24} = \frac{1}{2}a
\\
x_2 = \frac{8a-4a}{24}
& = \frac{4a}{24} = \frac{1}{6}a
\\\ \\
\because
a-\left(\frac{1}{2}a+\frac{1}{2}a\right)
& = 0
\\
\therefore
x \neq \frac{1}{2}a
\\\ \\
\because
a-\left(\frac{1}{6}a+\frac{1}{6}a\right)
& \gt 0
\\
\therefore
x = \frac{1}{6}a
\end{array}
$$
