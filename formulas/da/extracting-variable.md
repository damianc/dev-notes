# Extracting Variable from Expression

## Power

$$
\begin{cases}
\nabla = x^n
\\
x = \sqrt[n]{\nabla}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\begin{cases}
\nabla = n^x
\\
x = \frac{\ln\nabla}{\ln n} = \log_n\nabla
\end{cases}
$$

## Root

$$
\begin{cases}
\nabla = \sqrt[n]{x}
\\
x = \nabla^n \ \cup\ e^{n\cdot\ln\nabla}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\begin{cases}
\nabla = \sqrt[x]{n}
\\
x = \frac{1}{\log_n \nabla} = \log_{\nabla} n
\end{cases}
$$

## Logarithm

$$
\begin{cases}
\nabla = \log_n x
\\
x = n^{\nabla}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\begin{cases}
\nabla = \log_x n
\\
x = \sqrt[\nabla]{n} \ \cup\ e^{\frac{\ln n}{\nabla}}
\end{cases}
$$
