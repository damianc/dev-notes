# MathJax Matrixes

$$
\begin{matrix}
a & b
\\
c & d
\end{matrix}
$$

```
$$
\begin{matrix}
  a & b
  \\
  c & d
\end{matrix}
$$
```

$$
\det
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b
\\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
= ad-bc
$$

```
$$
\det
\begin{bmatrix}
  a & b
  \\
  c & d
\end{bmatrix}
= ad-bc
$$
```

## Types of Matrixes

| Type | Surrounding |
|--|--|
|`matrix`| |
|`pmatrix`|`(` with `)`|
|`bmatrix`|`[` with `]`|
|`Bmatrix`|`{` with `}`|
|`vmatrix`|`\|` with `\|`|
| `Vmatrix` | `\|\|` with `\|\|` |

### Custom Surrounding

$$
\left\langle
\begin{matrix}
a & b
\\
c & d
\end{matrix}
\right\rangle
$$

```
$$
\left\langle
  \begin{matrix}
    a & b
    \\
    c & d
  \end{matrix}
\right\rangle
$$
```
