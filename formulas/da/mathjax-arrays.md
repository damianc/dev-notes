# MathJax Arrays

## Nested Arrays

$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc}
  \left(\begin{array}{cc}
    a&b
    \\
    c&d
  \end{array}\right)
  &
  \left\\{\begin{array}{cc}
    a&b
    \\
    c&d
  \end{array}\right\\}_2^n
  \\
  \left\|\begin{array}{c|c}
    1&2\\
    \hline
    3&4
  \end{array}\right\|
  &
  \left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
    a&b&+
    \\
    c&d&x
  \end{array}\right]
\end{array}\right]
$$
