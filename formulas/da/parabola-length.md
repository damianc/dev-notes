# Parabola Length

## With polyline

$$
w = \frac{\vert x_2-x_1 \vert}{2}
$$

$$
\nabla = \frac{w}{s}
$$

$$
x_L = \min(x_1,x_2)
$$

$$
l = 2\left(\sum_{i=0}^{s} x_L + \nabla i\right)
$$

> $s \in (1;\infty)$ - number of samples

## With ellipse

l = \frac{\ell}{2}
