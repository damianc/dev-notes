# Integrals: Area and Length

## Area

- function: $f(x) = 2x-4$
- range: $\langle 4;6 \rangle$

$$
F \gets \int f(x) dx = x^2 - 4x
$$

$$
\int_4^6 f(x) dx = F(6)-F(4) = 12-0 = 12
$$

### Check

- trapezoid parallel sides: $f(4)=4$ and $f(6)=8$
- trapezoid _height_: $6-4=2$

$$
\frac{8+4}{2} \cdot 2 = 12
$$
