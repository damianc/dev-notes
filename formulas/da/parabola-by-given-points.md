# Parabola by Given Points

## Zeros and q

$$
w = \frac{x_2-x_1}{2}
\iff
x_2 > x_1
$$

$$
a = -\frac{q}{w^2}
$$

$$
f(x)=a(x-x_1)(x-x_2)
$$

## Span (s) and (p,q)

$$
w = \frac{1}{2}s
$$

$$
\begin{cases}
x_1 = p-w
\\
x_2 = p+w
\end{cases}
$$

$$
a = -\frac{q}{w^2}
$$

$$
f(x) = a(x-x_1)(x-x_2) = a(x-p)^2-w^2
$$

## Getting a/b/c and delta

$$
f(x) = a(x-x_1)(x-x_2)
$$

$$
= ax^2-a(x_1+x_2)x+ax_1x_2
$$

$$
\begin{cases}
a = a
\\
b = -a(x_1+x_2)
\\
c = ax_1x_2
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\Delta = b^2-4ac
$$

$$
= (-a[x_1+x_2])^2-4a^2x_1x_2
$$

$$
= ([x_1+x_2]^2-4x_1x_2)a^2
$$
