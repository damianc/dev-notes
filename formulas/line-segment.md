# Line Segment: Length and Center

Line segment has its:
- start at $(x_1, y_1)$
- end at $(x_2, y_2)$

## Length

$$
|AB| = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}
$$

it derives from _Pythagorean theorem_:

$C^2 = A^2 + B^2$  
$|AB|^2 = |AC|^2 + |BC|^2$  
$|AB| = \sqrt{|AC|^2 + |BC|^2}$

## Center

$$
S = (\frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2})
$$
