# Point on Circle by Angle

- circle: $(x-a)^2 + (y-b)^2 = r^2$
- angle: $\alpha$

$$
\Delta = \frac{1}{2}\pi - 2\pi \cdot \left( \frac{\alpha}{360} \right)
$$

$$
x = r \cdot \cos(\Delta) + a
$$

$$
y = r \cdot \sin(\Delta) + b
$$
