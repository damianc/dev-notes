# Line Transformations

> $f(x) = ax + b$

## Rotate (by $\gamma$ at $x$)

$$
\alpha = \arctan(a)
$$

$$
a' = \tan(\alpha + \gamma)
$$

$$
b' = f(x) - (x \cdot a')
$$

## Translate (by $\perp$ distance $d$)

$$
a' = a
$$

$$
b' = -d \cdot \sqrt{1 + a^2} + b
$$

## Translate (by $\vec{x}$ distance $d$)

$$
z = \frac{-b}{a} + d
$$

$$
a' = a
$$

$$
b' = -(z \cdot a')
$$

## Translate (by $\vec{y}$ distance $d$)

$$
a' = a
$$

$$
b' = b - d
$$
