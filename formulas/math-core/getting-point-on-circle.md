# Getting Point on Circle

- for circle $(x-a)^2 + (y-b)^2 = r^2$
- for point at angle $\vec{p}$

$$
\Delta = \frac{\pi}{2} - 2\pi \cdot \frac{\vec{p}}{360}
$$

$$
P_x = r \cdot \cos(\Delta) + a
$$

$$
P_y = r \cdot \sin(\Delta) + b
$$
